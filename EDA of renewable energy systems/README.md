<h2> Project Overview</h2>

<p>
This project presents a comprehensive <b>Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)</b> of renewable energy systems using a structured dataset containing technical, environmental, financial, and socio-economic indicators.
</p>

<p>
The objective is to evaluate renewable energy technologies across multiple dimensions including energy production, storage efficiency, greenhouse gas reduction, investment requirements, and employment generation.
</p>

<p>
The project demonstrates an end-to-end analytics workflow from data preprocessing and visualization to extracting actionable insights for sustainable energy planning and policy development.
</p>

---

<h2> Engineering / Business Problem</h2>

<p>
Renewable energy investment decisions require balancing multiple technical and economic factors.
</p>

<ul>
<li>Energy Generation Performance</li>

<h2> Results</h2>

<ul>

<li><b>Funding:</b> Public–Private Partnerships account for approximately <b>33.6%</b> of all projects.</li>

<li><b>Energy Production:</b> Geothermal systems achieve the highest average annual energy production.</li>

<li><b>Storage Capacity:</b> Biomass projects provide the highest storage capacity.</li>

<li><b>Storage Efficiency:</b> Most technologies operate between <b>60%–90%</b> efficiency.</li>

<li><b>Environmental Impact:</b> Biomass leads greenhouse gas reduction while Solar records the highest air pollution reduction index.</li>

<li><b>Financial:</b> Tidal requires the lowest investment while Wind receives the highest financial incentives.</li>

<li><b>Employment:</b> Renewable technologies create relatively consistent employment opportunities.</li>

</ul>

---

<h2> Key Insights</h2>

<ul>

<li>Balanced installed capacities enable fair technology comparison.</li>

<li>No renewable energy source dominates every performance metric.</li>

<li>Biomass excels in storage capacity and GHG reduction.</li>

<li>Geothermal provides the highest energy production.</li>

<li>Solar delivers the greatest local air quality improvements.</li>

<li>Wind receives strong governmental support.</li>

<li>Tidal projects require relatively low capital investment.</li>

<li>Storage efficiency remains consistently high across technologies.</li>

<li>Diversified renewable portfolios improve sustainability and energy security.</li>

</ul>
